Identity verification

The blue check, earned

Submit a photo of your SIRB, SRB or SID and a selfie — checked automatically, with a human review when needed. Once verified, your profile carries a blue check the crew and agencies can trust. Fully optional, and your ID photo is never shown to anyone.
