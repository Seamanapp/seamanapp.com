Everything a seafarer carries, organized

No more folders of photocopies and guesswork before an interview or an inspection. Your record is structured, always with you, and working for you.

📜

Certificates & scans

Every COC, GMDSS, BST and training certificate with its scan attached — and a clear valid / expiring / expired status at a glance.

🚢

Sea service record

Vessels, IMO numbers, tonnage, engine power, ranks and contract dates — the exact fields promotions and COC applications are judged on.

⏰

Expiry warnings

The app tells you before a certificate lapses — not the crewing officer after it already has.

🧭

Rank readiness

Pick your target rank and see exactly which requirements you already hold and which are still missing.

✈️

Works at sea

Fully offline-first. Add records mid-Pacific; they sync themselves the moment you find a signal.

🔒

Private by default

Nobody sees your records unless you grant it — and you can see and revoke every grant, any time.

“The seafarer owns their data. Everyone else borrows a view of it — with consent, for a purpose.”
