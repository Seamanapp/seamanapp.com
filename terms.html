The Service

Seaman App is a verified seafarer career-identity and community platform: a watch-day planner, rest-hours logging, a certificate and sea-service record, voyage and bridge tools, crew community features (posts, tips, Q&A, reviews, shared routes), field guides and courses, optional identity verification, and a recruitment marketplace connecting seafarers with manning agencies. The recruitment marketplace

Seaman App lets seafarers optionally make parts of their career record discoverable to manning agencies, set job preferences, and apply to postings. Agencies who wish to recruit through the Service must register and be approved. An agency can only see the parts of your record you have explicitly consented to share — medical certificates and identity documents are never shared automatically.

Seaman App facilitates introductions between seafarers and agencies; we are not a party to, and do not guarantee the outcome of, any employment, deployment or manning contract. We do our best to review an agency's licence information before approval, but this is not a guarantee of an agency's legitimacy, conduct, or compliance — exercise your own judgment, and verify an agency independently before entering any agreement with them.
