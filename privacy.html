Privacy Policy

Seaman App · seamanapp.com · Last updated 2 July 2026

Seaman App exists to keep a seafarer's career records safe and under the seafarer's own control, in line with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) and its IRR.

What we collect Why Who can see it Where it lives Your rights Retention Contact

What we collect

  • Your account email address.
  • The profile you choose to write: name, nationality, rank, headline.
  • The career records you add: certificates and their scans, sea-service history, achievements, and countries visited.

We only ever hold what you enter — there's no hidden tracking or third-party ad SDKs.

Why we collect it

Solely to provide the app to you: keeping your records in one place, warning you before certificates expire, computing your readiness for a target rank, and — only when you explicitly grant it — showing selected records to an organization you choose.

We do not sell personal data. We do not use it for advertising.

Who can see it

Nobody, by default. Your records are protected by row-level security in the database itself — not just by the app's screens. An organization can see a record only while an explicit, revocable consent that you granted is active. The app's “Who can see my data” screen lists every grant you've made and lets you revoke each one at any time.

Where it lives

On your device (so the app keeps working at sea, offline) and in our cloud database (Supabase, hosted in Singapore). Certificate scans are stored in a private bucket reachable only through short-lived signed links.

Your rights (RA 10173)

Access & correctionYour records are visible and editable in the app.
PortabilityRequest an export of your data at any time.
Objection & withdrawalRevoke any sharing grant in-app, instantly.
ErasureDelete your account and all data, permanently — here's how.

Sensitive actions — consent grants and revocations, deletions, verification decisions — are recorded in an immutable audit log, as the law expects of a responsible personal-information controller.

Data retention

Your data is kept for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, your login, records, and files are permanently removed; only the minimal immutable audit entry that a deletion occurred is retained, as required for accountability.

Contact

For any privacy request, question, or complaint: privacy@seamanapp.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the National Privacy Commission of the Philippines.
